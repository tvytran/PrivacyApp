⚖️ Legal Privacy Obligations

Based on your quiz answers, here are the laws most likely to apply to your project:

{% if "GDPR" in laws %}

🇪🇺 GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

  • Strongest privacy law for EU citizens
  • Requires legal basis for data, consent, data rights
  • Applies even if your company is outside the EU
  • Fines up to €20M or 4% of global revenue
{% endif %} {% if "HIPAA" in laws %}

🇺🇸 HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)

  • Applies to healthcare providers and vendors in the U.S.
  • Requires safeguards, breach notifications, and data access rights
  • Heavy fines for violations, up to $1.5M/year per category
{% endif %} {% if "CCPA/CPRA" in laws %}

🇺🇸 CCPA / CPRA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

  • Applies to businesses targeting or serving California residents
  • Gives rights to know, delete, opt-out of sale/sharing
  • Fines up to $7,500 per violation; allows consumer lawsuits
{% endif %} {% if "LGPD" in laws %}

🇧🇷 LGPD (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados)

  • Applies to any company processing data of people in Brazil
  • Very similar to GDPR — user rights, legal bases, DPO requirement
  • Fines up to 2% of Brazilian revenue (max R$50M)
{% endif %} {% if "General Guidance" in laws %}

📘 General Privacy Guidance

  • Be transparent about how you use data
  • Minimize what you collect and store
  • Encrypt sensitive information
  • Prepare for data access or deletion requests
